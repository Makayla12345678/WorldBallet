2025/26 Season

Upcoming Productions

  • November 1 8, 2025

     Procession

    Choreography: Bobbi Jene Smith and Or Schraiber

    In one of the most highly anticipated dance events of the season, visionary choreographers Bobbi Jene Smith and Or Schraiber unveil their first collaboration with The National Ballet of Canada, Procession, a groundbreaking work showing a bold new side of the companys artistry.

    Single Tickets on Sale September 23
    Subscriptions Now on Sale

    Learn More

  • November 14 21, 2025

     The Winter's Tale

    Choreography: Christopher Wheeldon

    From the acclaimed creators of Alices Adventures in Wonderland comes a tale of two kingdoms fractured by jealousy and reconciled in love. Choreographer Christopher Wheeldons The Winters Tale brings Shakespeares play to life with exceptional clarity and power.

    Single Tickets on Sale September 23
    Subscriptions Now on Sale

    Learn More

  • December 5 31, 2025

     The Nutcracker

    Choreography: James Kudelka

    Celebrating 30 magical years! Experience the wonder of the holidays with James Kudelkas The Nutcracker, as siblings Marie and Misha journey to the glittering palace of the Sugar Plum Fairy. Set to Tchaikovskys famous score, this beloved production is a glorious tribute to the imagination.

    Single Tickets on Sale September 23
    Subscriptions Now on Sale

    Learn More

  • February 27 March 6, 2026

     Flight Pattern / Suite en Blanc

    Choreography: Crystal Pite / Serge Lifar

    Canadas Crystal Pite partners once again with The National Ballet of Canada for the North American premiere of Flight Pattern, an urgent and emotional piece that shares the programme with Serge Lifars classical gem Suite en Blanc.

    Single Tickets on Sale September 23
    Subscriptions Now on Sale

    Learn More

  • March 13 22, 2026

     Pinocchio

    Choreography: Will Tuckett

    A reimagined Pinocchio returns to The National Ballet of Canada! Will Tucketts theatrical production brings a fresh perspective to the classic tale, whimsical stage design and a twist of Canadiana, with vibrant music by Paul Englishby.

    Single Tickets on Sale September 23
    Subscriptions Now on Sale

    Learn More

  • April 23 25, 2026

     Procession

    Choreography: Bobbi Jene Smith and Or Schraiber

    On Tour: Ottawa 
    National Arts Centre

    In one of the most highly anticipated dance events of the season, visionary choreographers Bobbi Jene Smith and Or Schraiber unveil their first collaboration with The National Ballet of Canada, Procession, a groundbreaking work showing a bold new side of the companys artistry.

    Learn More

  • May 29 June 4, 2026

     New Work by Jera Wolfe / Emma Bovary

    Choreography: Jera Wolfe / Helen Pickett

    One of Canadas most exciting dancemakers, Jera Wolfe presents his first mainstage work for The National Ballet of Canada alongside Helen Picketts Emma Bovary, which returns with new material after the world premiere in 2023.

    Single Tickets on Sale September 23
    Subscriptions Now on Sale

    Learn More

  • June 13 21, 2026

     MADDADDAM

    Choreography: Wayne McGregor

    Featuring original music by Max Richter, Wayne McGregors MADDADDAM returns to Toronto after a sensational 2022 premiere, with breathtaking visuals and sublime choreography inspired by Margaret Atwoods novel trilogy, Oryx and Crake, The Year of the Flood and MaddAddam.

    Single Tickets on Sale September 23
    Subscriptions Now on Sale

    Learn More
